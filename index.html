Chimaobi Sam Atu Foundation

  • Mission
  • Vision
  • Mandate
Chimaobi Sam Atu Foundation (CSAF) exists to create equal education, health and development opportunities for all irrespective of frontiers, Age creed and class.
To be a leading non-profit organization promoting education, healthcare, sports and youth development.
  • Pay special attention to youth education and development
  • Support and promote grassroots sports
  • Promote medical outreach targeted at women and children
  • Provide charity and relief items as the need arises
