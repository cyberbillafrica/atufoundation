Sam Atu, Founder

Africa has the youngest population in the world. There is, therefore, a need to invest heavily in the youth and prepare them for a knowledge-based economy that is driven by digital technologies. CSAF is committed to building a better future for young people in different areas of human endeavor, including sports and other productive ventures. Engaging young people in sports has proven to be one of the best ways to reduce youth restiveness. Sports not only promotes physical health but also instills valuable life skills such as teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. By providing access to sports facilities, training, and mentorship, we can empower young Africans to channel their energy positively.

Furthermore, CSAF's focus on youth development extends beyond sports to education, entrepreneurship, and skills training. By leveraging digital technologies, we can bridge the knowledge gap and equip young Africans with the skills required to thrive in the global economy. Through partnerships with organizations and stakeholders, CSAF aims to create opportunities for internships, apprenticeships, and job placements, ensuring that young people are equipped to drive Africa's economic growth and prosperity.
